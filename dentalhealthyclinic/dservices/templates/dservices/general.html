What Is General Dentistry?

General Dentistry serves as the foundation of oral care. Our general dentists are your primary dental care providers, offering services to patients of all ages.Here’s what we focus on:

Preventive Care:

  • Regular dental exams and professional teeth cleanings.
  • X-rays to detect hidden issues.
  • Education on proper oral hygiene practices

Basic Dental Services:

  • Addressing gum disease (gingivitis).
  • Oral cancer screenings..
  • Fluoride treatments.
  • Sealants to protect teeth.

When Should You See a General Dentist?

Twice a Year:
Schedule visits every six months for optimal care. Significant Changes: If you experience any oral discomfort or notice changes, seek an appointment promptly.
